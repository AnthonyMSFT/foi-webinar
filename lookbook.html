Homepage recording section concepts

Ten visual directions for the webinar recording section, laid out as mockups so you can compare the mood, hierarchy, and click-through treatment at a glance.

Jump to final concept
What this is

Make the recording feel like the main event, not an afterthought.

Each mockup keeps the same core message and LiveSend link pattern, but changes the section’s visual gravity: from cinematic to editorial, from ticket-like to layered and theatrical.

Use case: homepage spotlight after the webinar finished.

Concept 01

Cinematic band

A full-width, high-contrast band that treats the recording like a featured release. This is the strongest option if you want the section to feel like the natural next step after the hero.

Best for: maximum attention Mood: dramatic, premium
